Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

Your 30-day free trial gives you full access to all Premium features, including ad-free listening, offline downloads, and high-quality audio. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. If you cancel during your free trial, you won't be charged. After the trial, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How does the Family plan work?

The Family plan allows up to 6 people living at the same address to each have their own Premium account under one subscription. Each member gets full Premium features with their own personal login.

What audio quality does Premium offer?

Harmony Premium offers lossless FLAC streaming at 1411 kbps, which is CD-quality audio. We also offer high-quality 320 kbps AAC streaming for devices that don't support FLAC.

How many devices can I use with Premium?

You can use Harmony Premium on up to 5 devices simultaneously. There's no limit to how many devices you can register, but only 5 can be active at the same time.
