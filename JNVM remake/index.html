MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL

JAINARAYAN VIDHYA MANDIR, KANPUR

June 10, 2024
Dear Parents and Guardians,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you all to the new academic session 2024-25. As we embark on this new journey together, I reflect on our institution's proud legacy of academic excellence and holistic development.

Our consistent outstanding results in board examinations, along with remarkable achievements in sports, cultural events, and prestigious competitive exams like IIT-JEE, stand testament to our commitment to quality education. These accomplishments have firmly established us among the premier educational institutions in the city.

At our school, we blend modern educational practices with timeless Indian values. Our curriculum is carefully designed to not only impart knowledge but also instill strong moral character and ethical values - qualities that are indispensable in today's rapidly evolving world.

I urge you to actively participate in your ward's educational journey and encourage them to make the most of the opportunities our institution provides. Together, we can shape them into well-rounded individuals who will make meaningful contributions to society.

I look forward to your continued support and cooperation in making this academic year productive and memorable for all our students.

With warm regards,

Dr. Sant Ram Dwivedi
